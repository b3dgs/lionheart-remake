## Presentation

[Lionheart Remake](https://github.com/b3dgs/lionheart-remake) is a platform game that faithfully recreates the elements of the [original Amiga classic](http://hol.abime.net/894) (1992)
You are Valdyn, a Cat-Man empowered by the King to defeat Norka, a monstrous threat to the Kingdom. Llene, Valdyn's promised, has been kidnapped. The journey across 14 stages and 10 distinct worlds won't be easy.

*Note: This project is a use case for the [LionEngine](https://github.com/b3dgs/lionengine) game engine.*
